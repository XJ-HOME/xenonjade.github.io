FTC Disclosure Requirements

MANDATORY: You must clearly and conspicuously disclose your material connection (i.e., affiliate relationship) to XenonJade in ALL promotional content. This disclosure must be easily visible/audible and prominent, appearing before any affiliate links. Specific disclosure examples (e.g., "#ad", "#sponsored", "Paid partnership with XenonJade") will be provided in your affiliate dashboard and guidelines. Common examples include placing '#ad' or 'Sponsored by XenonJade' visibly in descriptions or on screen.

Content Usage Rights

You retain copyright to your original content. By participating in the program, you grant XenonJade a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use, reproduce, and share your XenonJade-related promotional content (e.g., reviews, social media posts) in our marketing efforts, always with appropriate attribution to you.

Tax Compliance (U.S. Affiliates)

This applies specifically to U.S. taxpayers. If your earnings from the XenonJade Affiliate Program reach $600 or more in a calendar year, you will be required to submit a completed W-9 form to us. Following this, XenonJade will issue a 1099-NEC form for your tax reporting purposes as required by the IRS.
